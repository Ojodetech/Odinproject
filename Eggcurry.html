Egg-curry

Description

It is a mixture of two delicacies, boiled eggs and tomato paste

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • Two tomatoes
  • A bulb onion
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the two eggs
  2. Remove the eggs after 15 minutes and remove the shells and slice half way through the eggs
  3. Slice the tomatoes and onion and fry them till they are pastry
  4. Dip the eggs into the tomato paste and add the seasoning
  5. Serve while hot with rice
