Chapati

Description

Chapati is a food that originally originated from India but got adopted by most kenyan homes and cooked as a ceremoniuos food

Ingredients

  • wheat flour
  • warm water
  • sugar,salt mix

Steps

  1. knid the flour till soft and not sticky
  2. roll the dough to a circular shape
  3. cut strips and roll then into bun shapes and from the bun shapes roll them into circular shapes.
  4. fry the circular dough till they have black spots all over
  5. serve while hot with a stew of your preference
