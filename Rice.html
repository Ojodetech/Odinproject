Yellow rice

Description

Rice is a cereal that is consumed almost everywhere on earth

Brown rice is just a simple modification of the rice that only changes the colour but also significantly alters the taste of the food itself

Ingredients

  • A cup of rice
  • water
  • food colour
  • a spoonful of oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add salt, oil and the food colour
  3. Reduce the amount of heat being used to minimum
  4. After 5 to 10 minutes remove and cover the food
  5. Serve while hot with fried chicken
